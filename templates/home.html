Create or join your team

This is one of the site's main pages, with 2 important functions:

  • “Create your team”: This button lets you create your team and takes you to page 2. We also have a "I don't have a team" option that redirects you to our discord where you can find teammates.
  • “Join a team”: This button gives you access to the list of teams already created but not yet complete, and whose status has been set as public by the team manager.

    • Create / Invite your team

    This page lets you invite (by clicking on the "Invite" button) any user/friend of the platform to join your team.
    if incomplete:

  • You can save your team (by clicking on the "Save" button), and finish it later, so you'll be redirected to page 3.
    • if complete:
  • You can create your team only if you've invited all 5 players (by clicking on the "Create team" button), so you'll be redirected to page 3.

    • Team lobby

    This page is considered the team's main page.
    Once the team is complete, it can search for a tournament (by clicking on "search a tournament") redirected Ranking page.
    There's also an "edit" option, accessible only by the team leader, which lets you add or remove team members.
    You can also see the points that each player has contributed during all the tournaments played with their team.

    Tournament lobby

    After searching for your tournament, you'll enter a lobby with 3 other teams.
    Over the course of 24 hours, each player on each team will accumulate points in an attempt to finish first in their lobby.
    The APIs provided by RIOT allow us to access the game history of the players on each team. To earn points for their team, players must play a maximum of 3 games, and their K/D/A will be counted to award more or less points to their team.

    Ranking page

    This league table lists the best teams on the platform (reset every month, along with all accumulated points). The top 8 from this final ranking will be selected for a monthly tournament, which will be broadcast and we will offer a cash prize to the winning team.

    Rank Explanation Page

    This page simply provides an explanation of each grade and the different points you need to accumulate to reach it.

    Team Profile

    This page is public and allows you to share your team's progress with all users.
